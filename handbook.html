Employee Handbook

Document ID: HR-POL-HBK-001  |  Effective: Immediately  |  Classification: Internal  |  Version: 11.0  |  Supersedes: All Prior Versions

This handbook constitutes the official policy guidelines for all employees of Tiny Riot Inc. All personnel are expected to have read, understood, and accepted these policies as a condition of employment. Tiny Riot Inc. acknowledges that several employees were not consulted during the drafting of these policies. This was intentional.

Policies are subject to update at any time. Updates will be communicated via the bulletin board on the Employee Portal. Grey will also know. He will not say anything. This is standard.

Questions may be directed to HR. HR responds between 10am and 2:45pm. HR does not respond after 3pm. Plan accordingly.

Section 1 — Workplace Conduct & Environment
1
Do not acknowledge the screaming from the Pine Barrens.
This is a professional wellness activity. It is not billable. It is not a safety incident. Jersey Devil will return shortly.
2
The break room is neutral territory.
All departments share the break room equally. No territorial claims are recognized. The snacks situation is covered under separate policy (see INC-1001). Label your items.
3
Chupacabra does not start things. This is the official position.
Employees are reminded that the official position reflects policy, not necessarily causation. Please do not ask HR to clarify this distinction further.
4
Kraken's door is always open. Consider that a warning.
Employees are not prohibited from visiting the Executive Director. Employees are encouraged to reflect carefully before doing so. The era of patience is ending.
Section 2 — Communications Policy
5
Do not reply-all.
This policy was added following INC-0991. It applies to all employees. It applies especially to one employee who has not been named here. She knows.
6
Mothman's predictions are logged but not discussed.
All predictive analytics outputs from the Strategic Foresight department are filed in the secure archive. Do not ask Mothman if he has seen something. He has seen something. This does not require discussion.
7
The 3am Slack messages are from The Rake. He is checking on you. Reply when you can.
These messages are not a security incident, a glitch, or cause for concern. They are from The Rake. He is checking on you. This is care. Please reply.
Section 3 — Colleague Protocols
8
If Nessie surfaces during a meeting, let her finish.
Nessie surfaces infrequently. When she does, she has something to say. It may take time. The meeting can wait. The meeting has always been able to wait.
9
Never ask Grey why he is documenting something.
Grey documents because documentation is necessary. The subject of the documentation is not your concern. Do not read the notes. This policy is for your own comfort.
10
Jackalope's expense reports are filed correctly. Do not investigate further.
All submitted expense reports have been reviewed and approved by finance. The reports are accurate. The underlying activities do not require further examination. This is the conclusion of the review process.
Section 4 — Facilities & Amenities
11
Soup left outside your door is from Bigfoot. Do not question it.
The soup is an act of care. It is not a hazard. It is not a prank. It has not been submitted through any formal gifting policy channel, and it does not need to be. Accept the soup. It will be good.
12
Do not ask what is in the basement.
This policy was added in Version 8.0 of the handbook. The previous policy addressed the basement directly. That policy has been removed. This is the policy now.
Section 5 — General Guidance
13
We are not a family. We are a highly dysfunctional ecosystem that has chosen, repeatedly and against considerable odds, to remain in the same lair.
This is the distinction that matters. Ecosystems are resilient. Ecosystems adapt. Nobody is leaving. — Management (probably)
14
HR does not respond after 3pm.
This is for everyone's safety. This includes HR's.
