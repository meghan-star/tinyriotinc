Company Overview

Document ID: BOARD-REC-001  |  Issued By: The Board  |  Classification: Restricted — Internal Distribution Approved  |  Status: Current

NOTE: This document was prepared by the Office of the Board. The Board does not field questions about this document. Questions submitted have been logged. The Board is aware.
Origins

Tiny Riot Inc. was established under the direction of the Board.

The founding date is not disclosed. The organization has operated continuously across multiple administrative eras, at least one period formally classified as restructuring, and one incident that remains under review. The founding mandate has not changed. The Board has not elaborated on the founding mandate.

Early organizational records are maintained by the Board. Access requests have been received. They have been logged. The Board will determine if and when access is appropriate. The Board has not made this determination.

Mandate

Tiny Riot Inc. exists to perform necessary work.

The scope of necessary work has expanded over time. This expansion was not always planned. In some cases it was the direct result of classified incident. The Board has reviewed each expansion and determined it appropriate after the fact. This is the standard process.

The mandate is ongoing. The Board does not anticipate this changing.

Organizational Structure

Tiny Riot Inc. operates under the direction of the Board. Day-to-day operations are managed by personnel. The Board monitors day-to-day operations.

Current departments include: Field Division, Strategic Foresight, Strategy, Research Division, Unplanned Outcomes, Human Resources, Legal & Compliance, External Affairs, and Labor Relations.

The existence of a Labor Relations department was not part of the original organizational design. The Board has accepted this. The Board did not have a choice. This has also been accepted.

A full organizational chart has not been produced. Several employees have declined to confirm their reporting lines. The Board is monitoring this situation. Grey is also monitoring this situation. Their findings are not shared.

Personnel

Current verified personnel: 9. The Board considers this sufficient.

Employee records are maintained in the internal directory. Several entries contain gaps, approximations, and at least two date ranges that predate conventional record-keeping. HR has flagged these discrepancies. The Board has noted HR's concerns. The Board does not consider geological timescales a records management issue.

New personnel are added at the discretion of the Board. The process by which this occurs is not documented. The outcomes have been satisfactory. The Board considers this sufficient explanation.

Operations

Tiny Riot Inc. maintains active operations across multiple locations, time zones, and in certain cases, geological formations. Remote work policy has been in effect longer than the formal concept of remote work. This is considered a legacy accommodation.

The organization operates continuously. There are no observed holidays. There are, however, several periods during which certain employees are unreachable by conventional means. These periods are logged. They are not considered absences.

Visitors to company locations should be aware that not all locations are accessible by standard transit. Several are not accessible by any documented method. Appointments are not available for these locations. The Board will be in touch if contact is required.
